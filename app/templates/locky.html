Locky Password Manager

Project information

  • Category: Android App
  • Project URL: Google Play | Official Website | GitHub

Locky is an easy to use password manager developed in native Android & Kotlin with minimalist components and that adheres to the material design. It's time to stop writing passwords on papers or use short unsafe passwords just for the sake of remembering it. You can save any passwords and bank cards offline on your own phone.
