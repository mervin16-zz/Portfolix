Locky Password Manager

Project information

  • Category: Android App
  • Project URL: Google Play | Official Website | GitHub Repo

Locky is an easy to use password manager developed in native Android & Kotlin with minimalist components and that adheres to the material design concept. It is time to stop writing passwords on papers or use short unsafe passwords just for the sake of remembering it. You can save any passwords,bank cards and much more offline on your own phone.
